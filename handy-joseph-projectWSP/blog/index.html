Wildlife Wednesday: Scientists Provide Great
Care for Animal Moms and Babies at Disney’s
Animal Kingdom

Giraffes, white rhinos, sable antelopes, gorillas and a red river hog were among
the animals that celebrated their first Mother’s Day this past Sunday at Disney’s
Animal Kingdom and Disney’s Animal Kingdom Lodge.

The Science Operations team at Disney’s Animal Kingdom is hard at work helping
provide great care for our animal moms and babies. Our team performs pregnancy
tests almost every day for animals in the park and at the Lodge.

We can predict the mother’s due date to help animal keepers prepare for
the delivery, and in some species we can even determine whether the baby
will be a girl or a boy!
